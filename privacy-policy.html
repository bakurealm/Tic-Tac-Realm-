Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 7, 2025

Tic Tac Realm ("us", "we", or "our") operates the tictacrealm.com website (the "Service"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service and the choices you have associated with that data. Your privacy is critically important to us, and this policy aims to be transparent and compliant with global privacy standards like GDPR and CCPA, as well as the requirements for advertising partners like Google AdSense.

1. Information Collection and Use

We are committed to a privacy-first approach. To use our Service, you are not required to create an account or provide any Directly Personally Identifiable Information (PII) such as your name, email address, physical address, or phone number.

a. Data We Collect Directly (Non-Personal)

  • Game State Data: To enhance your user experience, we use your browser's `localStorage` technology. This allows us to save your game statistics, such as win/loss/tie counts and your current level. This data is stored exclusively on your own computer or device and is not transmitted to our servers or any third party by us. It is used solely for you to track your game performance. Clearing your browser's cache or storage will permanently delete this data.

To keep our Service free, we partner with third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our website. These companies may use information about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you.
