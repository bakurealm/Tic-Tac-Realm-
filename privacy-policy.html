Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 7, 2025

Tic Tac Realm ("us", "we", or "our") operates the tictacrealm.com website (the "Service"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service and the choices you have associated with that data. Your privacy is critically important to us.

1. Information Collection and Use

We are committed to a privacy-first approach. To use our Service, you are not required to create an account or provide any Directly Personally Identifiable Information (PII) such as your name, email address, physical address, or phone number.

a. Data We Collect Directly (Non-Personal)

  • Game State Data: To enhance your user experience, we use your browser's `localStorage` technology. This allows us to save your game statistics, such as win/loss/tie counts and your current level. This data is stored exclusively on your own computer or device and is not transmitted to our servers or any third party by us. It is used solely for you to track your game performance. Clearing your browser's cache or storage will permanently delete this data.

b. Data Collected by Third-Party Advertising Services

To keep our Service free, we partner with third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our website. These companies may use information about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you.

Our advertising partner is `highperformanceformat.com`. You should consult their respective privacy policy for more detailed information on their practices as well as for instructions about how to opt-out of certain practices.

2. Cookie Policy

Cookies are files with a small amount of data which may include an anonymous unique identifier. They are sent to your browser from a website and stored on your device. Our site uses cookies for our advertising partners and for our cookie consent banner.

  • Consent: By using our Service and clicking "Accept" on our cookie consent banner, you agree to the placement of these cookies on your device.
  • Browser Controls: Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings preferences.

3. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Our Service is not directed at and does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under the age of 13.
