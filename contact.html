Contact Us

We value our players and welcome your feedback. Whether you have a question about the game, a suggestion for a new feature, a business inquiry, or have encountered a technical issue, please don't hesitate to get in touch. Your input is crucial in helping us improve Tic Tac Realm.

Primary Contact Method

For all inquiries, the best way to reach us is by email. We strive to respond to all messages within 48 business hours.

What to Include in Your Email

To help us assist you as quickly and efficiently as possible, please consider including the following information in your email:

  • For Technical Support: Please describe the issue in detail. Include the type of device (e.g., iPhone 13, Windows PC), the web browser you were using (e.g., Chrome, Safari), and any steps you took that led to the problem. Screenshots are incredibly helpful!
  • For Feedback & Suggestions: We're all ears! Let us know what you like, what you don't like, and what you'd love to see in future updates.

We are a small, dedicated team, and we read every piece of feedback we receive. Thank you for your interest and for helping make Tic Tac Realm the best it can be.
